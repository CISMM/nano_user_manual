The Analysis Menu hold tools to explore and compare your data quantitatively. 

Calculated data planes can remove slope from the surface, or allow comparison of data through simple arithmetic operations. The rulergrid, a grid of perpendicular lines laid over the surface, can be used to give you a sense of scale in the x and y directions, and to aid in estimations of measurement.  The measure lines can be used to measure height and distance.
