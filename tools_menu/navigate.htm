The Navigate dialog provides controls that can replace the Phantom in grab mode. By clicking in the center of the pads, and dragging towards the edges, you can rotate or translate the surface. The square pads control the X-Y translation and rotation, and the rectangular pads at the bottom control the Z rotation and translation.

Note: The motions produced by dragging on the pad are relative to the surface's axes, not the screen's axes. So if you have rotated the surface 180 degrees from its normal position, motions on the pad will move the surface in the opposite direction.
