Term Definition
AFM Atomic Force Microscope, also SFM
Automatic Lab Notebook Records all the data collected by the SPM during an experiment session, including scan and modification data. Normally ends in ".nms" extension. 
Grid
Shows the resolution of a scan, because the samples are evenly spaced, they occur on the intersections of the lines in a grid
The Rulergrid tool shows a picture of a grid on the surface, with various sizes and orientations.
NSOM Near-field Scanning Optical Microscope. Also NFOM, SNOM. 
Plane One layer of data from the SPM, collected during a scan. Examples are Topography-Forward, Topography-Reverse, Internal Sensor-Forward, Z Piezo-Forward.
Scan A set of data from the SPM, collected automatically as the tip moves back and forth across the sample. It can include one or more planes of data.
SFM Scanning Force Microscope, also AFM
SPM Scanning Probe Microscope, a superclass that includes AFM, STM, and NSOM.
Stream file Another name for an Automatic Lab Notebook file
STM Scanning Tunneling Microscope
Widget Any object on the screen you can use to control the program.
